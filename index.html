about me —

Greetings!

I'm Victor Uribe-Villicana, A front-end developer and I build websites from the ground up! Every project I take on is planned and thought out to meet the requirements of not only the client, but also to the purpose and functionality of the site. Keeping the purpose of the site in mind makes the design process more efficient which allows me to build a website that looks good across multiple devices and also capitalizes on the most important elements of the site. If there is a certain goal you want to meet through your website than look no further!

How do I do it?

Capturing the users attention, while staying true to the brand and the nature of the clients identity is one of my core standards when building websites. Endless cups of Premium Japanese Green Tea is also a standard part of my process. Humor aside on the technical side of things I build both the front and back end of websites in NodeJS. This is for a couple of reasons, the main reason being because everything is built using Javascript.
